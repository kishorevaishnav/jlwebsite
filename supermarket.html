Supermarket

SupermarketHQ is a marketplace that offers unique gifts and novel products from a curated list of world class designers, makers and artists.

SupermarketHQ lets buyers connect with various designers & sellers and request to customize products as per their taste and preference. They are happy to get products of their choice & design which gives them a feeling of buying a unique and self-designed product.

Sellers will have a comprehensive dashboard that provides details on sales activities. RF helped them to migrate from their homegrown solution to shopify. RF also built a custom backend to manage their vendors.

Tech Stack

Ruby on Rails, Postgres, Dwolla & PayPal integration
