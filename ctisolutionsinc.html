CTI Solutions Inc.,

CTI Solutions Inc is an independent software vendor who builds software that provides comprehensive solutions for Product lifecycle management.

Their solutions are used by fortune companies like Nike, Camuto, Jessica Simpson, SBG (Sequential), USPA, Mars chocolate and much more. These solutions have helped their clients in several product designs by managing their electronic resources in an easy, consistent, and cost-effective manner.

Understanding the diversity of clients and the fact that not all clients can adapt to one solution, RailsFactory played a strategic partner role & simplified the process onboarding the customers to the platform.

Apart from building the applications, RailsFactory proudly continues to be their long-term partner in supporting, maintaining the solutions and keeping their clients happy and satisfied.
