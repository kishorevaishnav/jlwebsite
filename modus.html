Modus

Modus is a pioneer innovative technology company that delivers intelligently designed and technologically advanced solutions to insurers, wireless carriers and fleet operators worldwide.

Truck and LMV owners can trace the vehicles and drivers and get information like vehicle health report, fuel cost reports, driving alerts, notification & limits, the routes involved in the journey of a destination and plan their operations accordingly. RailsFactory was involved in optimizing their solution and simplifying the onboarding process.

The app developed was installed in GPS devices integrated in the vehicles.

Tech Stack
